St. Louis Bowlwarz at Concord Bowl

About

Every Monday night, Concord Bowl hosts a tournament for Super Smash Bros. for the Nintendo Wii U.
Read the details below, and we hope to see you there!

Rules

Singles

2 Stocks, 6 Minute Timer, Double Elimination

Custom Moves are BANNED

DLC Characters are Legal

Items are set to OFF & NONE

Mii fighters using default guest profiles are legal for tournament play.
Anyone found using a non-guest Mii will be disqualified.
You may only use one set of Mii moves per set only. Switching Mii moves is not allowed.

Sudden death is resolved by % first. The person with the lower % wins. If both %'s are the same, a 1 stock 3 minute match will be played out to settle the tie.

There is no coaching between games.

Best of 3 for all sets except winners finals, losers finals and grand finals.
All finals will be best of 5.

Doubles

3 Stocks, 8 Minute Timer, Double Elimination

Team Attack is set to ON

Stock Sharing is LEGAL

Cost

$5 entry, $5 venue fee. Venue fee is waived if you bring a full setup (all characters unlocked, all DLC)

Prizes

First place: 60% of pot

Second place: 30% of pot

Third place: 10% of pot

When

6:00 - doors open for friendlies

7:00 - signups begin

7:30 - bracket begins

The format alternates between doubles and singles every week (each Monday has only one event). Please visit our Facebook page for an up to date schedule.

Where

Concord Bowl, 11801 Tesson Ferry Rd, St. Louis, MO 63128
